Economic Concepts Behind This Simulator

🏦

RBI Monetary Policy

The RBI repo rate is the rate at which commercial banks borrow from RBI. Higher rates → costlier loans → MSME credit constraint → slower expansion.

📉

Inflation Impact

Rising inflation erodes real profits. MSMEs with fixed contracts face cost-price squeezes when input costs rise faster than output prices, reducing viability.

🌱

Green Economics

Sustainability adoption reduces long-run costs via energy efficiency, attracts ESG-aligned credit, and aligns with India's Net Zero 2070 national goals for MSMEs.

📊

MSME & GDP

MSMEs contribute ~30% to India's GDP, employ 11 crore workers, and form 48% of exports. Their financial health is a direct macroeconomic indicator for national growth.
